# Code of Conduct

PositionTape uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1, as
the conduct standard for project spaces.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Accept responsibility and apologize to people affected by mistakes.
- Focus criticism on the work and its impact, not on people.
- Respect project boundaries, especially the open source/commercial boundary
  described in this repository.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions or review.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, temporarily ban
participants from project spaces, or permanently ban participants when conduct
violates this standard.

Security-sensitive or private conduct reports should use the private reporting
guidance in `SECURITY.md` rather than public issues.
